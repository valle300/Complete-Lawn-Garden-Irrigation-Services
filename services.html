DIY Sprinkler Head Repair

Follow this step-by-step guide to safely repair or replace a broken sprinkler head.

Tools Youâ€™ll Need

  • Replacement sprinkler head
  • Shovel or hand trowel
  • Pipe thread tape
  • Adjustable wrench

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Turn off the irrigation water supply.
  2. Dig carefully around the broken sprinkler head.
  3. Unscrew the old head and remove debris.
  4. Wrap thread tape on the new head.
  5. Screw in the new head and test the system.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Over-tightening the sprinkler head
  • Forgetting to flush debris before installation
  • Setting the head too low
When to Call a Pro: If you notice persistent leaks, low pressure, or damaged pipes, professional repair may be needed.
