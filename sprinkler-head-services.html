Sprinkler Head Services

Repair and installation of sprinkler heads to ensure proper coverage, water efficiency, and a healthy lawn.



Sprinkler Head Installation

We install all types of sprinkler heads, including spray heads, rotary heads, and adjustable nozzles. Proper installation ensures correct height, alignment, and water distribution for your landscape.

Sprinkler Head Repair

Broken, leaking, or clogged sprinkler heads can reduce system performance. We quickly diagnose issues and repair or replace damaged components to restore proper operation.

Sprinkler Head Repair & Installation Cost

Costs depend on the number of sprinkler heads, head type, and extent of damage. We provide clear, upfront pricing and free estimates before any work begins.



Common Sprinkler Head Problems


    Broken or cracked sprinkler heads

    Clogged nozzles

    Heads spraying in the wrong direction

    Sunken or tilted sprinkler heads

    Misting or uneven coverage


Do-It-Yourself Sprinkler Head Tips

Some minor sprinkler head issues can be handled on your own, such as cleaning clogged nozzles or adjusting spray direction.
