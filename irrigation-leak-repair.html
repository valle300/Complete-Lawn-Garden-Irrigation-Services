Irrigation Leak Detection & Repair

Locate and repair irrigation system leaks to prevent water waste, property damage, and high utility bills.

Irrigation Leak Detection

Finding the source of an irrigation leak isn’t always obvious. We use proven methods to locate hidden leaks, including pressure testing and zone inspections, to accurately diagnose the problem.

Types of Irrigation Leaks We Repair

  • Sprinkler system leaks
  • Underground pipe leaks
  • Valve box leaks
  • Drip irrigation leaks
  • Main line leaks


Irrigation Leak Repair Cost

Repair costs vary based on the location of the leak, depth of underground lines, and extent of damage. We provide honest pricing and free estimates before any work begins.

rrigation leaks can occur anywhere in your system, including sprinkler heads, underground pipes, valves, and drip lines. Signs You May Have an Irrigation Leak


    Unusually high water bills

    Pooling water or soggy soil

    Low water pressure in sprinkler zones

    Dry spots next to wet areas

    Mold or erosion around irrigation lines
